# MergeLine Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 2026-04-20_

This Privacy Policy describes how data is handled when you use **MergeLine**
(the "App"), a mobile game published by Tolga Sayan ("we", "us"). By using the
App you agree to the practices described below.

## Who the controller is

MergeLine is an independently developed mobile game. For any privacy question
or request, contact: **tolgasyn2@gmail.com**.

## What we collect

The App does not ask you to create an account and does not collect your name,
email, address, phone number, or contacts.

### Data we process ourselves

We store the following on your device only (not on our servers):

- Your best score, highest tile, games played, lifetime score total, and a flag
  that records whether you have completed the tutorial.

We use Apple's standard on-device storage (`UserDefaults` / the Godot engine's
`user://` directory). This data never leaves your device unless you back it up
through iCloud, which is controlled by your iOS settings.

### Data processed by advertising partners

The App displays banner, interstitial, and rewarded advertisements delivered by
**Google AdMob** (Google LLC). When ads are shown, Google may receive:

- A resettable advertising identifier (IDFA), if you grant App Tracking
  Transparency (ATT) permission.
- Device model, iOS version, language, approximate location (derived from IP
  address), the ad unit being requested, and crash / performance diagnostics.
- SKAdNetwork install-attribution postbacks that Apple sends to ad networks on
  our behalf. These postbacks are aggregated and do not identify you.

Google acts as an independent controller for data it receives through AdMob.
Its handling is described at:
<https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites>.

### Consent (GDPR / CCPA / UK GDPR)

Inside regions that require it (European Economic Area, United Kingdom,
Switzerland, California, etc.), the App shows a consent form powered by
Google's **User Messaging Platform** the first time you open it, and whenever
you reset consent. You can choose between personalized and non-personalized
ads, and you can reopen the consent form at any time by reinstalling the App
or clearing its storage.

### App Tracking Transparency

On iOS 14.5 and later, the system will ask you for permission to track your
activity across other apps and websites. If you decline, the App still works
and still shows ads, but only non-personalized ads are requested. Your choice
is recorded by iOS and can be changed at any time in
**Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking**.

## What we do **not** do

- We do not sell personal data.
- We do not profile children. The App is rated 4+ and AdMob requests are
  flagged with the "treat as G-rated" content filter.
- We do not use analytics SDKs beyond what is needed to serve ads and deliver
  crash diagnostics for the SDK itself.

## Children

The App is not directed at children under 13 (or the applicable minimum age in
your country). If you believe a child has provided personal information to us,
contact us and we will delete it.

## Security

On-device storage is protected by standard iOS sandboxing and file protection.
We do not operate servers that hold personal data from this App, so there is no
server-side dataset to breach.

## Your rights

Depending on your region, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or
restrict processing of your personal data. Because we do not collect personal
data on our own servers, most requests are best handled by:

- Clearing the App or resetting consent through iOS settings to remove locally
  stored preferences.
- Submitting data-subject requests for AdMob data directly to Google via the
  controls described at <https://policies.google.com/privacy>.

For anything else, email **tolgasyn2@gmail.com**. We reply within 30 days.

## Changes to this policy

When we change this policy in a material way, we will update the "Last
updated" date above and, where required, request renewed consent the next time
you open the App.
